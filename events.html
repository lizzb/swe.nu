Events

Each quarter, our section of SWE holds 1 major event, 1-2 general meetings, a few small social events, and outreach events. A few of these events are outlined below.

Major Events

Our major events consist of the following (click on event's title for more information):

STEP Program

A 3-Day Summer Technology and Engineering Program for 7-9th Grade Girls

Industry Day

Technical career fair

Career Day For Girls

Outreach event for middle and high school girls

Spring Banquet

A banquet honoring current SWE Members



General Meetings

Our general meetings are a time when our SWE members can gather for updates on SWE events, participate in some sort of programming, grab some free food, buy SWE t-shirts, and just hang out.

Social Events

For social events, we often try to organize lunches at the North Campus Einstein's Bagels. Sometimes we also have movie outings and are planning to hold more social events this upcoming fall when we return to school.

Outreach Events

We participate in various volunteer outreach events within the Northwestern and neighboring communities including Take Your Daughter to Work Day, Girl Scout Badge Activities and Engineering Week.
